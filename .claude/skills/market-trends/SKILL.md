# Market Trends Skill

## Purpose
Identify emerging trends, analyze market dynamics, and provide actionable intelligence for strategic decisions.

## Trend Analysis Framework

### Trend Identification Sources
- Industry publications and reports
- Conference themes and discussions
- Patent filings and R&D activity
- Venture capital investment patterns
- Regulatory and policy changes
- Social media and search trends
- Expert interviews and commentary

### Trend Validation Criteria
1. **Evidence**: Multiple independent sources confirm
2. **Momentum**: Growing, not isolated incidents
3. **Impact**: Meaningful effect on the market
4. **Durability**: Likely to persist, not a fad

## Signal vs. Noise Criteria
- **Signal**: Multiple sources, growing evidence, logical drivers
- **Noise**: Single source, static evidence, unclear drivers
