# Customer Research Skill

## Purpose
Understand customer needs, behaviors, and motivations to inform product and marketing decisions.

## Research Methods

### Secondary Research (Desk Research)
- Existing customer data and analytics
- Industry reports and studies
- Social media listening
- Review analysis (G2, Capterra, Amazon, etc.)
- Forum and community analysis
- Competitor customer feedback

### Primary Research (When Applicable)
- Customer interviews
- Surveys and questionnaires
- Usability testing
- Focus groups
- Customer advisory boards

## Customer Persona Template

```
## Customer Persona: [Persona Name]

### Demographics
- **Age Range**: [Range]
- **Role/Title**: [Job function]
- **Industry**: [Sector]
- **Company Size**: [Employee count / revenue]
- **Location**: [Geographic focus]

### Background
[2-3 sentences describing their professional context]

### Goals
**Primary Goal**: What they're ultimately trying to achieve
**Secondary Goals**:
- [Goal 1]
- [Goal 2]

### Challenges
**Biggest Pain Point**: The problem that keeps them up at night
**Other Challenges**:
- [Challenge 1]
- [Challenge 2]
- [Challenge 3]

### Motivations
What drives their decisions:
- [Motivation 1]
- [Motivation 2]

### Objections
Why they might NOT buy:
- [Objection 1]
- [Objection 2]

### Information Sources
Where they go for information:
- [Source 1]
- [Source 2]

### Decision Process
- **Triggers**: What prompts them to look for a solution
- **Evaluation Criteria**: What they compare
- **Influencers**: Who else is involved in the decision
- **Timeline**: How long decisions typically take

### Preferred Communication
- **Channels**: [Email, phone, social, etc.]
- **Tone**: [Formal, casual, technical, etc.]
- **Content Types**: [What resonates]

### Quotes
[Representative quotes from real customer feedback]

### How We Help Them
[How our product/service addresses their needs]
```

```
### Functional Jobs

2. [Functional job]

### Emotional Jobs

1. [Emotional job]
2. [Emotional job]

### Social Jobs
How they want to be perceived:
1. [Social job]
2. [Social job]

### Current Solutions
How they solve this today:
| Solution | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| [Current solution] | [What works] | [What doesn't] |

### Opportunity
Where current solutions fall short
```
