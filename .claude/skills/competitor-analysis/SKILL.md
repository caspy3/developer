# Competitor Analysis Skill

## Purpose
Conduct thorough competitive analysis that reveals strategic insights and actionable opportunities.

## Research Process

### 1. Identify Competitors
- **Direct**: Same product/service, same audience
- **Indirect**: Different product, same need
- **Aspirational**: Who you want to compete with
- **Emerging**: New entrants to watch

### 2. Gather Intelligence
- Company websites and product pages
- Press releases and news coverage
- Social media presence and engagement
- Job postings (reveal priorities)
- Customer reviews and feedback
- Industry reports and analyses

### 3. Analyze and Synthesize
- Identify patterns across competitors
- Find gaps and opportunities
- Assess competitive threats
- Develop strategic implications
