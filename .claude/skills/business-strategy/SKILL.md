# Business Strategy Skill

## Purpose
Develop clear, actionable business strategies that create competitive advantage and drive growth.

## Strategy Development Process

### 1. Situation Analysis
- Where are we now?
- What's working, what isn't?
- What's changing in our environment?

### 2. Strategic Choice
- Where will we play?
- How will we win?
- What capabilities do we need?

### 3. Execution Planning
- What actions will we take?
- How will we measure success?
- What resources are required?

```
### Weaknesses (Internal, Negative)
- [Weakness 1]
- [Weakness 2]

### Opportunities (External, Positive)
- [Opportunity 1]
- [Opportunity 2]

### Threats (External, Negative)
- [Threat 1]
- [Threat 2]

### Strategic Options
| | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|-----------|------------|
| **Opportunities** | SO: Use strengths to capture opportunities | WO: Address weaknesses to capture opportunities |
| **Threats** | ST: Use strengths to mitigate threats | WT: Address weaknesses to avoid threats |
```
