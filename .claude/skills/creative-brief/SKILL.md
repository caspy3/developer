# Creative Brief Skill

## Purpose
Create clear, inspiring creative briefs that provide strategic direction while leaving room for creative exploration.

## Brief Development Process

### Discovery Questions
1. What is the business objective?
2. Who is the target audience?
3. What is the single most important message?
4. What action do we want people to take?
5. What are the mandatories and constraints?

## Creative Brief Template

```
## Creative Brief: [Project Name]
Date: [Date]
Version: [Version]

### Project Overview
**Project Type**: [Campaign / Asset / Content]
**Deliverables**: [What we're creating]
**Timeline**: [Key dates]

### The Challenge
What business problem are we solving? What's the context that makes this necessary now?

### The Objective
What specific, measurable outcome does this creative need to achieve?

### Target Audience
**Primary**: [Who they are]
**Demographics**: [Age, location, income, etc.]
**Psychographics**: [Values, interests, behaviors]
**Current State**: [What they think/feel/do now]
**Desired State**: [What we want them to think/feel/do]

### The Insight
What human truth or tension can we tap into? What's the "aha" that makes this relevant?

### The Proposition
What is the single most compelling thing we can say? (One sentence max)

### Reasons to Believe
Why should the audience believe our proposition? What proof points support it?

### Tone & Personality
How should this feel? What personality should come through?
- [Adjective 1]
- [Adjective 2]
- [Adjective 3]

### Mandatories
Non-negotiables that must be included:
- [ ] [Mandatory 1]
- [ ] [Mandatory 2]

### Competitive Context
What else is the audience seeing? How do we stand out?

### Success Metrics
How will we measure if this worked?
- [Metric 1]
- [Metric 2]

### Inspiration & References
What existing work points in the right direction?

### Approvals
| Role | Name | Date |
|------|------|------|
| Strategy | | |
| Creative Lead | | |
| Client | | |
```

## Brief Quality Checklist
- [ ] Single, clear proposition (not multiple messages)
- [ ] Audience insight is actionable
- [ ] Objectives are measurable
- [ ] Mandatories are truly mandatory
- [ ] Room for creative interpretation
- [ ] Stakeholders aligned on direction
