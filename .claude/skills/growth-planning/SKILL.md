# Growth Planning Skill

## Purpose
Develop actionable growth strategies that identify opportunities and create clear paths to expansion.

## Growth Strategy Framework

### Growth Levers
1. **Market Penetration**: Sell more to existing customers in existing markets
2. **Market Development**: Enter new markets with existing products
3. **Product Development**: Create new products for existing markets
4. **Diversification**: New products in new markets (highest risk)
